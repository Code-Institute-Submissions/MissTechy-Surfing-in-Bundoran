Our Services

5 Days Junior Camp

We have 2 kids camp locations at Rossnowlagh and Tullan. A fun program for the week, consisting of beach games, water activities, surfing, and much more!

Families, Groups & Individual Lessons

Our Surf School is approved by the Irish Surfing Association, all lessons are fully insured and all instructors are qualified beach lifeguards.We have group rates available for groups of 8 or more.

Bundoran Surf Lodges

Free WIFI, breakfast and a wide screen TV. We offer a selection of single, double, twin and triple rooms as well as shared dorm rooms.

"Taking The Leap Of Faith"

Explore the wild coast of the North West and its unique craggy cliffs like you’ve never done before. We can now offer coasteering adventures with North West Coasteering.

SUP

Our SUP lessons/tours take place every day from May to September in flat water around Bundoran bay or Mullaghmore so ideal for beginners

Hungry & Thirsty?

After a day full of activities a BBQ is arranged for you and some pints. Then the party continues at the local pubs.
